Aviation Academy

What is Aviation Academy?

The Aviation Academy is a program offered to students at Seminole High School who are interested in a career pathway related to aviation.

  • The Academy follows the Maintenance pathway or the Avionics pathway.
  • Students have an opportunity to get their private pilot license through this program.
  • The maintenance pathway in aviation begins with the Private Pilot Ground School course. This allows students to learn the required information that all pilots must know for final aircraft inspection. All core flight safety knowledge including airport markings, weather, and reading charts are taught throughout this course. After Ground School, students gain knowledge and experience in the inner workings of aircraft through Aviation Maintenance courses.
  • The Avionics pathway introduces students to the high-tech electronics systems onboard an aircraft, including flight control, navigation and communications. In the avionics academy, students will gain practical knowledge of wiring, electronics, and the correct use and care of power tools.
  • Anybody can enter Aviation Academy, however it is highly suggested that you speak to a counselor or academic advisor before a student enrolls into the academy.

    • How can you apply?

  • To apply to Aviation Academy, register for Seminole High School regularly. Depending on your last name, please talk to your assigned counselor about Aviation Academy during the first week of school.
  • Follow these steps to get in contact with SCPS faculty: https://www.scps.k12.fl.us/district/departments/assignment-access/

    • Pros & Cons of Aviation Academy

    Pros:

    • Very beneficial to any student that wants to enter the aviation field.
    • Students get ahead with key information in their field, which will provide them an advantage for flight school.
    • Students have opportunities to earn certifications throughout the 4 year pathway of the program.
    • Traditional High School experience, you still are able to participate in electives/cores of your choice depending on what the student decides in Aviation Academy.

    Cons:

    • Newer program, meaning there may be difficulties with scheduling.
    • It is not beneficial for those who are not certain of being in the aviation field, meaning if you switch your profession, there is not many practical uses toward this academy.
    • Your support from counselors are not the best and it may take a while to get your questions answered.
